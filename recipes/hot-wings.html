Tasty Buffalo Wings!

These wings will be perfect to fire you up before the game!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 pounds of chicken wings, flats and drums from the frozen section
  • 2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 tsp. crushed red pepper flakes, if you don't have pepper flakes, you can use paprika
  • ½ cup Frank’s RedHot® Buffalo Sauce
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons honey
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Season the un-frozen wings with salt and pepper and set aside. In a bowl, add the hot sauce, garlic, melted butter, honey, and red pepper flakes.
  2. In a gallon-sized sealable bag, combine the chicken wings and the sauce. Seal the bag and mix the sauce and chicken until well combined. Refrigerate for a couple of hours to let the chicken marinate.
  3. In a glass baking dish, arrange chicken pieces in single file. Cover the chicken with the remaining sauce.
  4. Preheat your oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit. Place your chicken on the top rack of the oven and bake for 10-15 minutes. Remove the chicken.
  5. Transfer your chicken to a broiling pan and broil 2-4 minutes on each side or until your meat thermometer reads 160 degrees Fahrenheit.
  6. Remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest. Hint: if your chicken seems dry, brush the wings with more sauce. Serve it with some celery, carrots, blue cheese, ranch dipping sauce, or with our favorite potato wedges.
